# encode\(\)/decode\(\)

Converting from bytes to str and back

`bstr=b'qwerty'`

`type (bstr)`

`<class 'bytes'>`

--------------------------------------------------

`str=bstr.decode('utf-8')`

`type(str)`

`<class 'str'>`

---------------------------------------------------

`bstr2 =str.encode('utf-8')`

`type(bstr2)`

`<class 'bytes'>`
